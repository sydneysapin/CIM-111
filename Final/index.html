A Modern Day Yoga Flow

There was once a time where yoga meant creaky traditional rooms, dead silent spaces, and 75+ minute practices that saw human pretzel-like poses one after another that only the creator of Yoga himself could probably complete successfully. But this is 2019 on the brink of a completely new decade. Why should yoga be stuck in the past if nothing else in our modern-day hustle lifestyles is? Say hello to the next generation of yogi’s and their cutting edge take on the classic flow. Bright lights, heated rooms, music you love, practices that are as long as is efficient . Say hello to the modern day flow.

About Us: The Story of Miami's First Blacklight Yoga Studio

Introducing GLOflo, the cutting edge capstone of modern day yoga and pilates practices.

Established in Miami as the first yoga studio that offers classes set to the backdrop of blacklight ambiance lighting, GLOflo takes the vibrancy and youthfulness off the 305 and transforms it into your new favorite fitness experience. For 45-60 minutes, GLOflo’s “(flo)Guru’s” lead you through a steamy and challenging workout set to the motivating beats of your favorite tunes of today.

Be it spicy mat pilates, zenful yoga, or a combination of both, GLOflo is the unique and edgy workout you’ve been craving since forever.

First Time at GLOflo?

    Before

      Make sure your (flo)’s secured by booking your spot in class before you arrive to the studio. First timers and any level of yoga or pilates experiences are more than welcome!

      Class cancellations are free of charges up to 4 hours before the class begins. A fee of $5 will be charged if not.

      Towels, mats, and waters can be rented at the front desk. However, if you have that one special mat you can’t live without, feel free to bring it to class!

    During

      If you are more than 5 minutes late arriving to class you will not be allowed in. No exceptions for those who “slow the (flo)”!

      If you are a first timer, please arrive to the studio 10-15 minutes before your first class so we can set you up with everything you need for a great class.

      NO CELLPHONES ALLOWED DURING CLASS. The only GLO we want to see during class is from your clothes reflecting the backlights!

    After

      Our studio offers complimentary lockers and shower stalls in our bathrooms if you need a quick rinse before heading out.

      Ask our front desk about daily merch and membership deals happening!

      Spread the vibes! Feel free to reach out to the GLOflo team or just leave us a friendly review on Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, or Yelp
